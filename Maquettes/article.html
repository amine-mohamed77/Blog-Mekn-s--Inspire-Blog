Tales from Bab Mansour

Zineb El Idrissi
Oct 28, 2025
Meknes

At the grand gate of Bab Mansour, stories echo between mosaics and arches. Locals share moments, travelers pause, and the evening breeze carries the scent of mint and clay.

As the sun settles, colors shift to deep terracotta and gold. Musicians gather, and a rhythm forms that blends the past with the present. The gate becomes a stage for everyday poetry.

These are the threads that weave Meknes: small acts of kindness, rituals of tea, the soft hum of markets, and a city that listens.

Walk slowly, listen closely, and the city will tell you a new story each day.
